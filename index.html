SlaQuers – Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

SlaQuers is a private party-game app created for a small group of friends. We respect your privacy and only collect the minimum information needed to run the game.

Information We Collect

SlaQuers collects only the following:

  • Display name – the name you enter when joining a game
  • Optional avatar
  • Gameplay data – answers, scores, rounds played
  • Device type (Android/iOS) for technical performance only

We do not collect:

  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts
  • Advertising IDs
  • Analytics or tracking data

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Join a game
  • Synchronise gameplay between devices
  • Display your name, avatar and score to other players in the same game

We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

How Your Data Is Stored

Game data is stored temporarily on our backend (Supabase) so games can function. Once a game ends, the data is cleared or becomes anonymous.

Third-Party Services

SlaQuers uses the following service:

  • Supabase – for hosting game sessions and real-time multiplayer functionality.

Supabase may process basic connection metadata such as IP addresses as part of normal operation.

Children’s Privacy

SlaQuers is intended for adults and not designed for children under 13.

Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect data. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.
